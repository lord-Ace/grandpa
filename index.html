About Joseph Azu

Joseph Ikemefune Azu, fondly known as JoeJoe, was born on October 20, 1948, in Jos, Plateau State. A passionate learner, he pursued education in computer programming and business administration, earning multiple certifications and a B.Sc. from the University of Lagos. He built a distinguished career in Nigeria’s tech industry, working with top firms like NCR, ICL, and MBM. Joe was a devout Catholic, a loving husband to Veronica, and a proud father and grandfather. He was deeply involved in community and social groups, known for his joyful spirit, integrity, and commitment to truth. His legacy lives on through his family, faith, and the many lives he touched.
